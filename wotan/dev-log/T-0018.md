## Context
Show how party programmes and election manifestos translate into positions on concrete policy questions. **Not** an SVT-data replica — built on open party programmes/manifestos.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Own question bank of ≥ 20 policy questions across ≥ 10 topic areas (skola, kriminalitet, migration, energi, välfärd, klimat, EU, övervakning, landsbygd, arbetsmarknad).

- [ ] "This question simplifies the following" note per question.
- [ ] Evidence panel + scatter/matrix rendering.
- [ ] At least 8 parties covered.
